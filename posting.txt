    About this Unit:

    The objective of the Division of Behavioral Health Analytics, Epidemiology, and Evaluation (BHAEE) is to apply a broad range of knowledge and analytical skills to rapidly integrate, create, and share data and findings between multiple sources to ensure Agency leadership, external stakeholders, and the public have access to accurate and timely information and data to achieve objectives. This team draws on analytical skills in leadership, policy, program
    management and public health communication.

    The Health Information Technology (HIT) Division develops and implements all of the BHA’s information technology efforts. This division also collaborates with the Analytics, Epidemiology, and Evaluation Division on data strategy. Between both divisions, they are tasked with modernizing the BHA’s data strategy. The Health Information Technology Division collaborates with other Departments on data and IT initiatives. This division played a key role, along with Colorado Digital Services, in implementing the BHA’s Care Directory, OwnPath.

    About this Position:

    This position is a Temporary Aide position with an anticipated end date of 9 months after hire date.  This position will be working approximately 20 to 40 hours per week.

    Summary of Job Duties:

        Data Collection and Cleaning: Collect and clean data from various sources, ensuring data accuracy, completeness, and consistency. Develop data cleaning routines and scripts to automate data cleaning processes.
        Data Analysis: Analyze data using statistical methods and data visualization tools to identify patterns, trends, and insights. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and execute data-driven solutions to business problems
        Reporting: Develop and generate reports and dashboards to provide insights and recommendations to key stakeholders. Create visualizations and data summaries to effectively communicate complex data insights.
        Documentation and Communication: Document data analysis processes, methods, and results. Communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders through presentations, reports, and other means of communication
        Process Improvement: Identify opportunities to improve data collection, processing, and analysis processes. Develop and implement new data analytics tools and techniques to increase efficiency and accuracy
